Process

Three steps, then it's just your board.

01

Create an account

Sign up with an email and password. Boardly opens straight to an empty board, ready to fill in.

02

Write your first tickets

Type a task and hit enter, or press Ctrl+K. Give it a category so it's easy to spot later.

03

Drag it through

Move tickets from To do to In progress to Done. Everything saves and survives a refresh.

FAQ

Questions people actually ask

Is my data private?

Yes — every task row is tied to your account and locked with database-level row security, so no other user can ever read or edit it.

Do I need to install anything?

No — Boardly runs entirely in the browser. Sign up and your board is ready.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes — the whole interface, including drag-and-drop, is built to work down to a small phone screen.
